
# ЁЯУК Advanced Tableau Sales & Customer Dashboard

## ЁЯУМ Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive Sales & Customer Dashboard built in Tableau using a retail sales dataset. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product performance through interactive visualizations.

Designed for business decision-makers, the dashboard enables users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), analyze trends over time, identify top-performing products and customers, and uncover actionable insights for strategic planning.

---

# ЁЯОп Business Problem

Organizations generate large volumes of transactional sales data, making it difficult to monitor business performance and identify growth opportunities through raw spreadsheets alone.

This dashboard addresses this challenge by transforming raw sales data into an interactive business intelligence solution that enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor business performance
- Track revenue and profitability
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Compare monthly performance
- Identify top-performing products and customers
- Support data-driven decision-making

# ЁЯУК Features / Highlights

- Sales Dashboard and Customer Dashboard in one project
- KPI tracking for sales, profit, quantity, customers, and orders
- Monthly trend analysis with comparison against previous year
- Customer distribution analysis by number of orders
- Top 10 customers by profit
- Subcategory-wise sales and profit comparison
- Interactive filters and drill-down visuals for better analysis
- Professional dashboard layout for executive reporting

---

# ЁЯУМ Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer the following business questions:

- Which months generated the highest sales?
- Which product sub-categories generate the highest profit?
- Which products contribute the most to total revenue?
- Who are the top 10 most profitable customers?
- How has customer acquisition changed over time?
- What is the average sales per customer?
- How many repeat customers does the business have?
- Which product categories require business attention?
